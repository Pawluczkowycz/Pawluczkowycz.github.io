Doctor Website Mockup

Ashburn Pediatrics Center is fictitious doctor's website. The idea is loosely based on several pediatrician's sites. The site is responsive and includes a small CSS animation: the sun drops down into the site and subtly pulses sunshine.

The site includes Google Web fonts, jQuery, a Google Map and a touch of CSS animation.
