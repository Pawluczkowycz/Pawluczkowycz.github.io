E-commerce Website Mockup

Brookbury Drive is a fictitious e-commerce site selling handmade leather bags. The idea is based on several sites I've seen devoted to selling hand- and custom-made ideas. My design employs a minimal design, using simple photography and colors, thin lines and white space for each section.

Like my Mom's Organic Market site, this site uses the Responsive Grid System, and includes fonts from Font Awesome, a jQuery slideshow and CSS3 styles, and media queries for a responsive layout.
