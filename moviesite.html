Movie Website Mockup

Into the Jungle, A Mother and Son Adventure is a fictitious movie site advertising a children's motion picture. The idea is based on a family trip to Puerto Rico. The design features full-page photography with a subtle vignetting for a cinematic affect.

The site includes Google Web fonts, jQuery, and a touch of CSS animation.
