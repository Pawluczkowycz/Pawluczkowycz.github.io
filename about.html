About Me

My name is Andrew Pawluczkowycz and I'm a designer. I love looking at and thinking about graphic design. Despite years of working as a biochemist, I believe my natural talents are in art and design; that's why I'm here.

Happily, I was given the opportunity to formally study graphic design, and teach myself front-end web design (so far HTML, CSS, and jQuery, with a little bit of: OOCSS, native JS, Git, UX, Accessibility, Performance, and anything else I have a chance to read).

Also, I am dedicated to my family, eating and drinking well, life-long learning, and standing outside as much as possible.
