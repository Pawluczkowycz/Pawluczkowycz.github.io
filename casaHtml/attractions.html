Local Attractions

Take advantage of our private hiking paths at Casa Yunque Ecolodge. These paths lead to scenic get-aways with clear pools for swimming and cascading waterfalls. A short walk up the road, brings you to...

... the South entrance gate of the El Yunque Rainforest, complete with a number of different hiking trail options. Our massage therapist is available by appointment.
