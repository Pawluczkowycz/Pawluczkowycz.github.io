Casa Yunque Restaurant

Casa Yunque Restaurant is half a mile (ten minute walk or two minute drive) down the road from the ecolodge. Guests can sit at tables or at the long open-air counter that overlooks...

... the forest with absolutely breath-taking views. Shades of green foliage, swaying in the breeze, and striped silver by the rays of the sun is a beautiful sight to behold.

You will be mesmerized by the sweeping vistas, while enjoying one of our famous mojitos. The restaurant is the ideal location to appreciate the beauty of El Yunque and a savory meal.

While waiting for your meal, you may want to shoot a game of pool or relax and converse with fellow guests. Soft latin music complements the restaurant's peaceful 'ambiente'.

The Menu

Our menu offers Puerto Rican dishes with generous portions such as pastelon, mofongo, tostones, and of course, rice & beans. The first three items mentioned are made from plantains....

... Eggplant parmesan, rice Florentine, and pasta with vegetables in a cream sauce are among our vegetarian offerings. Our creamy seafood risotto is a favorite, as is the butter fried fish and shrimp with pasta or mofongo.

We have some old fashioned deserts like chocolate layer cake or brownie with a scoop of vanilla ice cream. We also have guava shells in heavy syrup with white cheese, homemade flan, and fruit sorbets.
