Casa Yunque Ecolodge

A Bed and Breakfast in the El Yunque Rainforest of Puerto Rico

Our rooms and rates

Unlike most hotel chains, we provide a variety of different room options. Each of our rooms has its own unique charm. Top floor rooms offer private balconies with spectacular views and bottom floor rooms give...

... guests easy access to the Casa Yunque hiking trail. We have a handicap accessible room on the main floor. There is also one room with a private porch below the Casa Yunque Restaurant. Please see below for room details (including pictures!), how to make a reservation, and our cancellation policy at the bottom of this page.

The Ecolodge has ten rooms and our nightly rates range from $110 (lower level rooms) to $125 (upper level rooms) for two guests plus 9% tax. There is a $25 charge per extra person per night. In order to really enjoy one’s stay and take in all that El Yunque Rainforest has to offer, we encourage our guests to stay more than one evening. There is an extra room cleaning fee of $20 for “one-night” guests.

Check-in hours are between 1:00 and 9:00 PM. Check-out is at 11:00 AM. Please call ahead of time if you plan on checking in after 9:00 PM.

Casa Yunque Restaurant

Casa Yunque Restaurant is half a mile (ten minute walk or two minute drive) down the road from the ecolodge. Guests can sit at tables or at the long open-air counter that...

... overlooks the forest with absolutely breath-taking views. Shades of green foliage, swaying in the breeze, and striped silver by the rays of the sun is a beautiful sight to behold.

You will be mesmerized by the sweeping vistas, while enjoying one of our famous mojitos. The restaurant is the ideal location to appreciate the beauty of El Yunque and a savory meal.

While waiting for your meal, you may want to shoot a game of pool or relax and converse with fellow guests. Soft latin music complements the restaurant's peaceful 'ambiente'.
