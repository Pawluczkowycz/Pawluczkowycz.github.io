Contact Us

Phone: 787-321-4321

Email: info@casayunque.com

We take reservations via phone or email. To make a reservation we ask that you provide us with the exact check-in and check-out dates, the number of people in your party, whether you would like an upper or lower level room, and a credit card number with expiration date. We accept Visa and Mastercard. If you are contacting us by email this last piece of information can be sent in two emails for security purposes.

Directions

We are within the Proclamation Boundary of the El Yunque national Forest, which is a 28,000 acre nature reserve. There are 7 municipalities that ring the forest. We are on the South side in the municipality of Naguabo, one hour and fifteen minute drive from San Juan. Our physical address is State road #191, km 25.
