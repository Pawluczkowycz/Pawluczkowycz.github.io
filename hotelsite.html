Hotel Website Mockup

The Casa Yunque Ecolodge is a fictitious hotel located in a rainforest. The idea was based on a vacation my family and I took to the beautiful island of Puerto Rico.

The overall layout is based on Chris Coyier's CSS-Tricks article "Don't Overthink It Grids": a responsive, flexible grid was built using percentages and media queries. Shown below is the desktop version. I've included a Google Web font and a little jQuery and CSS3 styles to complement imagery and layout.
