Organic Grocery Store Website Mockup

Mom's Market is a fictitious organic food and grocery store. The idea comes from a local market's site, which I believe looks a little dated. For the overall look I chose bright colors and a simple layout to suggest an warm and inventing atmosphere for the store.

The site was built using Graham Miller's Responsive Grid System, a small css framework I stumbled upon. I've added a jQuery slideshow and Font Awesome icons.
